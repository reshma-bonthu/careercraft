Computer Application Courses: After 12th, Syllabus, Eligibility, Fees, Scope 2024

Computer Application Courses are available in certification, diploma, and degree program format; either as a stand-alone course or with Web Design or Graphic Design specialization. The curriculum of computer application courses focuses on advanced/ intermediate systems to build software by using other programming languages such as HTML, JavaScript, and C++.

Diploma in Computer Applications, Diploma in Computer Programming, BCA, BCA Computer Science, and MCA are the best computer application degree courses. Christ University, the University of Madras, and Parul University are among the top colleges to offer computer application courses.

Admissions to these colleges are usually based on entrance exams such as LPU NEST, IPU CET, CUET, etc. Computer applications certificate courses are available both online as well as offline mode. Various online computer application certification courses are available on platforms like Udemy, edX, and Coursera.

Certificate ­Computer Application Courses: Admission Process

  • Students who have completed their schooling under the 10+2 system in any Stream (Though the Science Stream is Preferable) with securing at least 55% to 60% Marks in aggregate from recognized boards of education are eligible for the above-mentioned certification course in the field of IT.
  • Online certificate courses can be pursued by anyone, there is no such bar on age, gender, or stream.
  • For Advance Certification, Students must have Bachelor’s degree or have any other equivalent certification/degree in the same field with a minimum of 40% to 50% aggregate marks and can also be eligible for it.
  • No Entrance Tests are conducted in Certification Courses.
