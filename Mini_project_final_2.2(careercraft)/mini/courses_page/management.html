Management Courses: After 12th, Online, Fees, Syllabus, Colleges, Jobs, Salary 2024

Management courses offer a comprehensive understanding of business disciplines like Finance, Economics, and Marketing, along with in-depth knowledge of management theories and their relationships with various industries. Popular courses include new-age specializations or electives, which teach modern skills and knowledge. When selecting an online program, students also consider job opportunities, personalized mentor interactions, and resume services.

MBA, BBA, and PGDM are popular management courses available in various specializations like Hospital Management, Hotel Management, Aviation Management, Event Management, etc. Management Courses are offered at Certificate, Diploma, and Degree levels (Bachelors's, Masters's, Doctorate).

IIM Indore, IIM Lucknow, and Xavier School of Management are Top Management Colleges in India. Hospitality, Banking, Financial Marketing, Sales and Marketing, and Human Resource Management are the top MBA Specializations in these colleges.

After completing Management Courses, students get jobs in various fields such as Business Development Managers, Financial Managers, Marketing Managers, Event Managers, Production Managers, etc. The average salary for these roles ranges from INR 7 LPA to INR 10 LPA in India.

Management Courses After Graduation

Business administration is the main topic of study in the three-year UG management courses. Additionally, students are taught managerial and entrepreneurship skills. Certificate Management Courses

All the certificate programs in Management Courses are offered by the Institute or University through both Online and Offline modes. Both the modes of the courses are very popular among students and the duration of the courses depends on the syllabus delivered.

  • Certificate Courses in Management are specially designed for candidates who want to get additional skills or sharpen up their skills.
  • A certificate course is specially designed in such a way that working professionals can pursue them along with their job.
  • In the case of online courses, there is no fixed schedule and courses are self-paced.

Students who are looking to learn a specific management subject or topic can opt for these short-term certificate courses. The average salary after certificate Management Courses for beginners is INR 2,00,000 to INR 3,00,000 per annum.

Bachelor in Management Courses

Bachelor’s degrees in management are one of the most opted Management Courses as most of the students want to opt for a full-time degree course. These kinds of bachelor courses need an effective campus education and placement opportunity, though nowadays most of the Management Courses are being conducted online due to the coronavirus spread.

  • The duration of Bachelor Management Courses is 3 years.
  • Admissions for this course are done after 10+2 through entrance exams at the national level and state level entrance test. Many colleges also give merit score-based admission.
  • Bachelor's degree in management helps the student to build a proper foundation for learning through different sets of programs.

A bachelor's management course is a great start to building a long-time career in management. The average salary for the bachelor's Management Courses is INR 3,00,000 to INR 8,00,000 per annum, but this sometimes depends on the skills of the candidate and the institute.

Master in Management Courses

Master Management Courses are pursued by students who have already completed their bachelor's and want to go for further studies in that particular field. A candidate can opt for both general courses or specialized courses.

  • The duration of PG Management Courses is 2 years for most of the courses. One of the most popular courses MBA is generally offered for 2 years but some institutes offer this course for 3 to 5 years.
  • Master courses help to enhance a student’s skills and increase their in-depth knowledge in a particular field.
  • Admissions for this course are done through various entrance exams. One of the most demanded entrance exams for these master Management Courses is the Common Admission Test (CAT).
  • Apart from CAT, there are many other popular entrance exams such as MAT, XAT, CMAT, SNAP, TISSNET, IBSAT, GMAT, etc.

The average package of master Management Courses in India is INR 8,00,000 to INR 18,00,000. It can be more for experienced professionals.

Management Courses Jobs and Careers

A management degree provides graduates with a broad understanding of business, finance, economics, and marketing, as well as a variety of practical skills and work experience, making them highly sought after by graduate employers and graduate training schemes.

Managers typically earn more than their employees. You'll notice that I didn't use the word "worker." Most skilled workers are classified as exempt, which means they do not receive overtime pay. Exempt employees, on the other hand, do not punch in, can take time off without being asked, and are managers themselves.

As management jobs are one of the most demanding options nowadays, employers look for candidates who are advanced with all the required managerial skills and the latest trends.

Some of the most popular job roles after this management course, average salary, and skills are tabulated below.

Job Profile Job Description Average Annual Salary

BTech FAQs

Q.Is it possible to learn Management courses Online?

Ans.Yes, there are a lot of certifications and PG Diploma courses conducted online. All of them are mentioned in the article above.

Q.What is the total duration of a Management Course?

Ans.he duration of the Management course depends on the level of study. For UG, it is 3 years, at PG level it is around 1-2 years, diploma 6 to 12 months and for Certification, the course is for 3-6 months..

Q What is the cost to pursue a Management course?

Ans.Fees for Management courses may vary as per the institute. There are online certifications available, but the individual fee depends on the level of the course and where you pursue the course from. The average course fee is ranging from INR 75,000 to 10 lakh. .

Q.Shall I get a scholarship to pursue management courses?

Ans. Yes. Some universities or institutes offer a scholarship for students and even some online course providers also offer scholarships.

Q.What is the average course fee for MBA in Hospital Management in India?

Ans.The course fee for MBA in Hospital Management ranges from 60,000 to 7.5 lakh.

Q.Is pursuing the course PGDM more profitable than pursuing an MBA?

Ans.Both courses are profitable. The basic difference between the courses is PGDM is more practical than an MBA.

Q.Is there any institute that provides PGDM distance education?

Ans.Yes, there are many institutes such as IGNOU, Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning (SCDL), Nalanda Open University provides distance education. .
