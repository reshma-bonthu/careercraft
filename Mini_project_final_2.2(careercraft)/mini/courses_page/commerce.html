How to Study Abroad After 12th Commerce?

Certain prerequisites must be met before planning to study abroad after 12th commerce. To begin, here are some of the prerequisites:

  • A student visa is required in order to study and live in the country
  • Students must have completed their 12th grade from a recognized institution with good grades in the course of interest..
  • The student should have enough money to cover tuition and living expenses during the study period. Bank statements, scholarship information, and so on must be provided as evidence.
  • Students must be 18 years old when applying for courses to study abroad after completing their 12th grade..
  • English proficiency exams such as IELTS, PTE, and TOEFL, as well as subject-specific exams such as SAT, GRE, and GMAT, must be taken. If the university has an entrance examination
