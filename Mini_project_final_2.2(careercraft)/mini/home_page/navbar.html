Science Courses: After 12th, 10th, Jobs, Online and Colleges 2024

`

Science Courses are a combination of various subjects opted by candidates after 10th or 12th grade out of the subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology. Science Courses have always been the most sought-after courses in India.

The most popular Science Courses in India are degree courses such as Bachelor of Science, Master of Science, Diploma in Science, Doctorate (Ph.D.) in Science, etc. A candidate must opt for a combination of Science Courses After the 10th in order to be eligible for the degree courses mentioned above. The average course fee for admissions into Science Courses ranges from INR 5000 to INR 10,00,000.

Top Science Colleges in India that offer Science Courses in numerous disciplines are IITs, Delhi University, Fergusson College, IGNOU Delhi, Assam University, etc. Entrance Exams accepted for Science Courses admissions are UPSEE, BHU UET, IPU CET, KCET, IIT JAM, etc.

After completing Science Courses, the candidates are flooded with numerous job opportunities such as Scientist, Professor, Nutritionist, Marine Engineer, Environmentalist, Clinical Dietician, etc. The average starting salary offered to these professionals goes around INR 3,00,000 to INR 10,00,000 per annum

Certificate in Science Courses Admissions

The admission process for Certificate Science courses is mostly a basic one. Some courses do not even have any prerequisites.

  • To enroll yourself for any of the online courses, you will have to go to the official website of the course provider and register yourself for the course.

  • However, to get yourself admitted to an offline course, you will have to submit your academic documents.

Doctorate in Science Courses

Doctorate Science Courses are research-based courses that mainly have a duration of around 5 years.

  • The Doctorate Science course will allow the students to discover and work on the latest concepts of science.
  • The students, after completing their course, will be able to carry out research activities and also teach at reputed organizations.
  • Upon successful completion of a doctorate course in Science, the candidates will annually earn around INR 5,00,000 to INR 10,00,000.

The courses are meant for those candidates who want to build a career in teaching. Students interested in research also go for doctorate courses.

Science Courses FAQs

Ques. Which is the best specialization for a Science course?

Ans. Specializations in Science include fields like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Environmental Science, Biotechnology, Computer Science, and more, each offering unique career opportunities.

Ques. Are Science courses available online?

Ans. Yes, many Science courses are available online through platforms like Coursera, edX, Udemy, and others, offering flexibility and accessibility.

Ques. Which is the most popular Science course available for students?

Ans. Popular Science courses include Computer Science, Biotechnology, Environmental Science, Physics, Chemistry, and others, depending on current trends and career demand.

Ques. What is the average tuition fee for Science courses?

Ans. The average tuition fee for Science courses varies widely depending on the institute and course type, ranging from affordable options to higher-end institutions.

Ques. Which is the best institute to study Science in India?

Ans. Top institutes for Science in India include IITs, NITs, DU, AIIMS, and other reputed universities known for their quality education and research facilities.

Ques. Is choosing Science in 10+2 a good career choice?

Ans. Yes, choosing Science in 10+2 opens up a wide range of career opportunities in fields like engineering, medicine, research, IT, and more, offering stability and growth.

Ques. Do I need to study Science in 10+2 to take up a Science course?

Ans. Generally, studying Science in 10+2 is a prerequisite for many Science courses at the undergraduate level. However, specific requirements may vary by institution and program.

Ques. What are the popular entrance tests for Science courses?

Ans. Popular entrance tests for Science courses in India include JEE Main, NEET, AIIMS MBBS, BITSAT, and state-level exams like MHT-CET, KCET, and others.

Ques. What is the average salary of Science students?

Ans. The average salary of Science students varies based on the specialization and industry, with starting salaries typically ranging from INR 3,00,000 to INR 8,00,000 per annum.

Ques. Can I study Science abroad?

Ans. Yes, many universities abroad offer Science courses to international students. Requirements may include language proficiency tests, academic qualifications, and visa processes.
